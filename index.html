The Meaning of Samriddhi

Founded in 2025

Building the future of organic spices with traditional values

"Samriddhi" means prosperity and growth in Sanskrit. What started as a small family tradition in an Assam village has grown into a mission to bring the purest, most authentic masalas to kitchens across India.

Our founders grew up watching their grandparents grind spices by hand, understanding that true flavor comes from patience, tradition, and respect for nature. Today, we honor those values while embracing modern quality standards.
