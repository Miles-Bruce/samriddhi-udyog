Our Story

Rooted in Tradition, Growing with Pride

The Beginning in Assam

Our journey began in a small, serene village nestled in the lush landscapes of Assam, where generations of our family cultivated the land with love and respect for nature. What started as a humble endeavor to preserve the authentic flavors of our heritage has blossomed into a mission to bring pure, organic spices to kitchens across the nation.

What does "Samriddhi" mean?

In Sanskrit, "Samriddhi" means prosperity, abundance, and flourishing. It represents our vision—to bring prosperity not just to our business, but to the farmers we work with, the communities we serve, and every family that welcomes our spices into their homes.

Our Values

  • Tradition & Purity: We honor time-tested methods passed down through generations
  • Family Values: Every product is crafted with the care we'd give our own family
  • Continuous Growth: From a village enterprise to a national FMCG brand, we're expanding with integrity
